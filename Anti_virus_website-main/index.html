TechWave Antivirus FAQ

Find answers to common questions about our security solutions

What makes TechWave Antivirus different from other security solutions?

TechWave Antivirus uses advanced AI-powered threat detection combined with real-time behavioral analysis to protect against both known and emerging threats. Our unique TechWave Shield technology creates multiple layers of defense, ensuring comprehensive protection with minimal system impact.

How often are virus definitions updated?

Our security team updates virus definitions every 15 minutes to ensure you're protected against the latest threats. Additionally, our cloud-based threat intelligence network provides real-time protection against zero-day exploits between definition updates.

Does TechWave Antivirus slow down my computer?

No, our optimized scanning engine is designed for minimal system impact. In benchmark tests, TechWave Antivirus uses up to 30% fewer system resources than competitors while providing superior protection. You can also schedule scans during idle times to further reduce any potential impact.

What devices and platforms does TechWave Antivirus support?

We offer comprehensive protection for:

  • Windows (7, 8, 10, 11)
  • macOS
  • Android and iOS mobile devices
  • Linux servers (enterprise edition)

All versions can be managed through our unified TechWave Security Dashboard.

How does the TechWave VPN feature enhance my security?

Our integrated VPN provides bank-grade encryption for all your internet traffic, protecting your data from hackers on public Wi-Fi and preventing ISP tracking. The VPN is automatically activated when connecting to unsecured networks, and we maintain a strict no-logs policy to ensure your privacy.

What should I do if I suspect my device is infected?

Follow these steps immediately:

  1. Run a full system scan with TechWave Antivirus
  2. Disconnect from the internet to prevent data exfiltration
  3. Contact our 24/7 support team for emergency assistance
  4. Use our Rescue Disk tool if the infection prevents normal operation

All premium subscribers receive priority malware removal support.

Can I use TechWave Antivirus for my business?

Absolutely! Our Enterprise Protection Suite offers centralized management, advanced threat analytics, and specialized protection for servers and network endpoints. We provide volume licensing discounts and dedicated account managers for businesses with 10+ devices.
